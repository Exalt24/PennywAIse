About PennywAise

PennywAise is your all-in-one personal finance assistant. From the moment you link your accounts, it automatically categorizes every transaction—so you can see exactly where your money is going without lifting a finger. Set custom budgets for groceries, bills, entertainment or anything else, and get real-time alerts whenever you're approaching your limits. Want to save for a vacation, a new gadget, or an emergency fund? PennywAise's goal-tracker breaks down your target into manageable milestones, sends friendly reminders, and even suggests small adjustments you can make this week to stay on pace. Thousands of happy users have already taken control of their finances—reducing overspend by up to 30%, hitting savings targets months early, and finally feeling confident about their money. Ready to join them? Let PennywAise guide you to better spending, smarter saving, and greater financial peace of mind.

Features

  • AI-Powered Financial Coaching

    Get intelligent suggestions on where to save, which subscriptions to trim, and personalized tips based on your spending patterns—instantly.

  • Secure Login & Registration

    Sign up, log in and out with ease. Each user has their own secure account and personalized data—backed by Django’s battle-tested authentication.

  • Add/Edit/Delete Entries

    Effortlessly record income or expenses with title, amount, date & notes—and modify or remove them anytime.

  • Smart Categorization

    Organize your spending—Food, Travel, Bills—and gain clarity. Choose from preset categories or add your own.

  • Monthly Summary & Charts

    Pie charts of category spend & bar charts comparing income vs. expense—so you instantly see where your money goes each month.

  • Intuitive Dashboard

    One glance shows total income, total expenses, remaining balance, plus quick links to add entries or view history.

  • Budget Setting & Alerts

    Define monthly budgets per category or overall—get notified the moment you approach or exceed your limit.

  • Export Your Data

    Download your entire history as CSV—filter by date or category—and analyze in Excel or Google Sheets.
